OVERVIEW

Composar is an app that simulates an immersive audio experience throughout a story. It enables users to experience mono (where all sounds are mixed into a single channel) and stereo audio (where sounds are separated into two different channels to create a sense of width and depth), as well as 3D positional audio tracks that remain at the assigned location in 3D space, represented by an augmented reality object, and vary in sound based on the user's distance and location.
