OVERVIEW

Dexter is a unique online multiplayer game. Unlike other online games, it requires players to gather onsite. Divided into teams, players collaborate to complete various tasks in order to survive in the camp. These tasks involve diverse technologies and require unique interactions with devices as well as with other players.


EFFORTS AND INVOLVEMENT

  • Developed, co-designed, and engineered front-end user experiences with animations using SpriteKit and Haptics.
  • Collaborated to innovate an offline multiplayer gaming system, leading to 7 unique methods of device interaction within the game.
