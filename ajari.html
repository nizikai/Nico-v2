OVERVIEW

Ajari is a simple Guitar Learning app. It is designed to assist beginners in mastering the fundamentals of playing the guitar. It also offers a user-friendly interface with intuitive chord visualizations and a clear, structured learning path. This app offers beginner-level courses tailored for users who are just starting their guitar journey. With step-by-step guidance, guitar learners can easily grasp chord formations and strumming techniques, ensuring a smooth learning experience.


EFFORTS AND INVOLVEMENT

  • Created an app for rapid production and easy recording of guitar chord sounds dataset, saving 100+ future hours of dataset creation.
  • Built, implemented, and user tested a machine learning model for live guitar chord sound classification with average accuracy of 83,45.
  • Conducted user research throughout the design process by interviewing and gathering feedback from guitar learners and teachers.
