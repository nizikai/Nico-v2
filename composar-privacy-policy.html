Composar Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Composar handles user information. We want to assure you that your privacy is our top priority, and we are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of any information provided to us.

Data Collection

Composar does not collect or store any personal information from its users. We do not access your device's data, contacts, or any other personal information.

Audio Usage

Composar utilizes device audio features solely for the purpose of delivering an immersive audio experience within the app. No audio data is recorded, stored, or shared externally.

Location Information

Composar does not access or track your location. The app's 3D positional audio features are solely based on virtual positioning within the app and do not involve real-world location data.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of December 1, 2023. Any changes to the policy will be updated on this page.
