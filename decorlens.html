OVERVIEW

DecorLens is an iOS app prototype that utilizes Augmented Reality (AR) to provide potential buyers with a lifelike view of furniture products. The app prototype allows users to virtually place furniture within their own spaces, offering a clear visualization of how each piece fits and complements their home. This approach eliminates the uncertainty often associated with static product photos, enabling a more informed and confident shopping experience.
