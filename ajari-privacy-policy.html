Privacy Policy for AJARI

Your privacy is important to us. It is AJARI's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any recordings or sounds from your use of the AJARI app. All learning progress is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device, including your learning progress, is used solely for the purpose of enhancing your experience within the AJARI app. We do not access, use, or share this information with third parties.

Chord Strumming and Exercise

While AJARI utilizes machine learning for chord detection during the chord strumming and exercise feature, please note that the actual audio recordings are not stored or transmitted to our servers. The chord detection is performed locally on your device.

Real-Time Feedback

During the real-time feedback feature, AJARI may process the audio input from your device to provide feedback on the correctness of the chords played. However, no audio recordings are stored or sent to our servers.
